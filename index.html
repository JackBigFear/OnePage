BIG FEAR

Entry Level — EP • Out 09 May 2026

London trio BIG FEAR return with Entry Level — Their second EP with Charlie Andrew (Alt-J, Wolf Alice).

Big Fear—like Big Pharma, Big Tech, Big Dick— is a vaguely menacing and all-encompassing boogeyman; a reckoning with fear itself. The London trio explores the point where terror curdles into apathy. Self-reflexively dystopian and operatic, Big Fear’s songs are synth-throbbing generational anthems served with an almost ironic pomp.
